# Project Instructions: AI News Portal

## SEO & Reporting
- **Responsibility**: OmniClaw is responsible for all SEO monitoring, reporting, and indexation checks.
- **Workflow**: Do NOT prompt the user to check Google Search Console manually. All verification and status checks must be handled via automated protocols or direct API interactions (where available).
- **Maintenance**: Periodically re-verify sitemap.xml accessibility and robots.txt compliance.

## Content Management
- **Cadence**: Maintain high activity with frequent updates on trending AI news.
- **Standards**: Every new page must include:
    - JSON-LD NewsArticle schema.
    - Open Graph / Twitter meta tags.
    - Descriptive alt text for images.
    - Internal linking to related articles.
